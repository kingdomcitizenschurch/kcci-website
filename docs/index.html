About Us

Kingdom Citizens Church International (KCCI) is a vibrant, Spirit-filled church committed to impacting lives globally through the love of Christ. We are a family of believers dedicated to spiritual growth, community service, and excellence in worship. Our mission is to raise leaders, foster discipleship, and extend God’s kingdom across nations.

At KCCI, we believe in empowering every member to discover their purpose, serve passionately, and experience transformative spiritual encounters through worship, teaching, and fellowship.
