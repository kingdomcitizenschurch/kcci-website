About Kingdom Citizens

Our Mandate

"The hour has come to liberate the world from all oppressions of the devil through the preaching of the Word of Faith"

For over 12 years, Kingdom Citizens Church International has stood as a beacon of hope and spiritual growth in Abeokuta. Founded by God through Pastors Sam and Felicia Balogun, our church is more than a building—it's a vibrant family and a movement dedicated to raising purposeful believers.

We are located at Wole Iredele Street, Abiola, Abeokuta. Our mission is to empower individuals to discover their God-given identity and destiny as true citizens of His Kingdom.

Visit Us
Pastor Sam Balogun

Founding Pastor

Pastor Sam Balogun is a visionary leader and the founding pastor of Kingdom Citizens Church International. With over a decade of ministry experience, he is passionate about empowering believers to discover their God-given purpose and impact the world through faith, prayer, and practical kingdom living.

Pastor Mrs. Felicia Balogun

Co-Pastor & Wife of the Founder

Pastor Mrs. Felicia Balogun is a dynamic co-leader and the heart of Kingdom Citizens Church International. She is committed to nurturing the congregation through teaching, mentorship, and counseling, creating a loving environment that strengthens families and builds a spiritually grounded community.
