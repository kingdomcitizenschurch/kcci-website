About Us

Kingdom Citizens Church International (KCCI) is a vibrant Christian community committed to spreading the love of Christ, promoting spiritual growth, and fostering community development. Founded by Pastor Sam Balogun, our mission is to empower believers and cultivate leaders who impact society with faith, integrity, and service.

Our services, ministries, and outreach programs are designed to meet the spiritual and practical needs of individuals and families at every stage of life.

Pastor Sam Balogun

Founder and Senior Pastor of KCCI. Pastor Sam is a visionary leader, committed to nurturing spiritual growth and community development. With years of ministry experience, he inspires believers to fulfill their God-given purpose.

Membership

Becoming a member of Kingdom Citizens Church International means joining a family committed to growth, service, and kingdom impact. Members receive spiritual guidance, mentoring, and participation in all church programs and ministries.

Our membership process is simple and welcoming. Whether you are new to faith or a seasoned believer, our team will guide you through joining our vibrant community.
