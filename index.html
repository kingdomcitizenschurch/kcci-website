About Kingdom Citizens

For over 12 years, Kingdom Citizens Church International has stood as a beacon of hope and spiritual growth in Abeokuta. Founded by God through Pastors Sam and Felicia Balogun, our church is more than a building—it's a vibrant family and a movement dedicated to raising purposeful believers.

We are located at Wole Iredele Street, Abiola, Abeokuta. Our mission is to empower individuals to discover their God-given identity and destiny as true citizens of His Kingdom.

Mission: To restore lost souls and destinies back to Christ’s kingdom through preaching the word of faith.
